# LittleSeed Money

LittleSeed Money is an evidence-based personal finance intelligence platform for ordinary Americans in the age of AI capitalism.

Internal codename: **BetterThanDave**

Official product name: **LittleSeed Money**

Tagline:

```text
Small steps. Wise growth.
```

Mission:

```text
End financial illiteracy in the age of AI capitalism.
```

## Product Identity

LittleSeed Money helps ordinary people understand money, make better financial decisions, and grow what they have been given with wisdom and purpose.

The product is built on the belief that small beginnings can grow into something meaningful when planted wisely, nurtured patiently, and used for good.

## What LittleSeed Money Is

LittleSeed Money is an evidence-based financial decision intelligence platform.

It combines:

- Manual household financial input
- Official economic and tax reference data
- Historical market data
- Financial models and simulations
- Explainable AI
- Financial education
- Long-term behavior coaching

## What LittleSeed Money Is Not Merely

LittleSeed Money is not merely a budgeting app.

Budgeting, spending analysis, asset dashboards, and account aggregation may become important product layers, but the core mission is larger:

> Help people understand money, compare financial decisions, and build wealth with clarity, wisdom, and purpose.

## Development Rule

Before building any feature, check:

1. Does this support the mission?
2. Is this current phase, or should it go to the backlog?
3. What data does it need?
4. What model or calculation supports it?
5. How will the result be explained to a normal user?
6. What are the assumptions, risks, and limitations?
